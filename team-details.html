Biography

Rev. Nonso Stanley Anene is an inspirational young team leader with exceptional leadership skills and has deliberately strived towards excellence in all that he does. He has passion for healthcare, community development service, social work and philanthropy.

He currently serves as the Director of Administration at the University on the Niger Teaching Hospital (formerly known as the Iyienu Mission Hospital, Ogidi). He has a training in Leadership & Management in Healthcare from the University of Washington USA and an Executive Diploma in International Healthcare Administration with LaPlage Metaverse CourseWare. He has an MSc in Applied Microbiology from the Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, a Bachelors in Theology (B.Th) from the Paul's University Awka, a Diploma in Management Sciences (Dip.MS) from the Institute of Management Specialists, Warwickshire, England, and an MBA in-view with the National Open University of Nigeria. He is a Certified Management Consultant (CMC).

He has served in the leadership of several teams such as: pioneer State Coordinator of the Young African Leaders Initiative (YALI) Network Anambra state; elected Assistant State Secretary of the National Youth Council of Nigeria (NYCN) Anambra chapter; former Country Director of the Global Youth Assembly (GYA) for Nigeria after serving for some months as the National Vice-Chair; pioneer State Chairman of the Anambra Young Professionals Network (AYPN).

He is the Founder of the RibbonCare Volunteer Network through which he has coordinated several Community Medical Outreaches to rural and underserved communities. He is the founder of the Olive Sickle Cell Initiative – an NGO focused at reducing the incidence of the sickle cell disorder in Nigeria.

Nonso has won several awards and recognitions amongst which are: Fellow of the Institute of Management Specialists (FIMS); Fellow of the Institute of Management Consultants (FIMC); and International Youth Award by the Jamie Pajoel International. He bagged the State Honours' Award from the National Youth Service Corps (NYSC) Delta State during his NYSC in 2010.

Nonso is a member of many organizations including the FRSC where he serves as a Special Marshal and was the Special Assistant on Health to the immediate past State Coordinator of the Anambra State FRSC Special Marshals. He is also a member of the Police Community Relations Committee (PCRC) Ogidi Division.

Nonso is an ordained priest in the Anglican Communion and worships at St Peter's Parish Nkpor-Agu (New Covenant Archdeaconry) of Diocese on the Niger; and is married to his beautiful wife, Mercy Nzubechukwu Anene and are both blessed with a beautiful daughter, Chizaram Michelle Chimnora Anene. In his free time, Nonso likes reading, teaching, talking, writing, thinking and listening to music. He is multi-talented, as he plays the piano, bass guitar and saxophone. He is also a natural-born public speaker and teacher with exceptional skills in multi-tasking. 'Prof', as his close friends would usually call him, yearns towards greatness and excellence in whatever he does. He likes to break and set records. He is not discouraged by age or youthfulness. He believes in service – to God and humanity. "Servant-leadership" is his hallmark in leadership.
