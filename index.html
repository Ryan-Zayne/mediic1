About Iyienu Mission Hospital

One of Nigeria's Leading Healthcare Institutions

Since 1907, Iyienu Mission Hospital has been at the forefront of healthcare delivery in Nigeria. We are committed to providing quality, affordable, and accessible healthcare services to all, irrespective of sex, race, social status, or religion.

Our mission is to ensure that no one is denied good healthcare because of who or what they are. We constantly strive to be on top in training healthcare personnel for the health system.
