WHO WE ARE

About Iyienu Mission Hospital

Iyienu Mission Hospital is one of Nigeria's leading healthcare institutions with an outstanding reputation for delivering excellent clinical services. Since our inception under the current management (1998), we have remained at the forefront of providing and delivering quality patient-centered, responsive, and technology-driven care across various locations in Nigeria to both citizens and foreign nationals.

Our integrated health services are wide-ranging, from cardiology and ophthalmology to radio-diagnostics and laboratory services. Our team of highly qualified medical professionals is equipped to provide a comprehensive range of treatments, including minimal access procedures, neonatology, ICU care, and other general medical and surgical services.

We are proud to be an accredited center for specialist training in various medical fields, offering internships to medical doctors, radiographers, optometrists, and pharmacists.
